About PixelResize

PixelResize is a professional-grade, browser-based image optimization platform designed for creators, developers, and businesses who prioritize privacy and speed.

Our tools allow you to resize, compress, crop, and convert images instantly using your device's local processing power. No files are ever uploaded to a server.

Security by Design: Your images never leave your machine. Everything is processed in the browser RAM for 100% privacy.

Why Professionals Choose PixelResize?

  • 100% Privacy First: Zero server uploads. Your data, your machine.
  • Lightning Performance: Local execution avoids upload/download latency.
  • SEO Optimized: High-fidelity compression inspired by Google's Squoosh for better CWV.
  • Completely Free: No accounts, no subscriptions, no limits on processing.

Whether you're building a website, managing social media, or submitting official documents, PixelResize provides the specialized tools you need without compromising your security.
