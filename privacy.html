Privacy Policy

At PixelResize, we believe your data belongs to you. Our commitment to privacy is built into the architecture of our platform.

PixelResize never stores, copies, or transmits your images to external servers.

Our Data Protection Principles

  • No Image Uploads: We do NOT upload your images to any server. All processing happens locally in your browser.
  • No Data Storage: We do NOT store your images, files, or any personal processing data.
  • Local Browser Processing: All image manipulation occurs entirely within your browser's RAM. Once you close the tab, the transient data is gone.
  • No Account Required: You can use all our tools anonymously without providing an email address or creating an account.
  • Analytics & Improvements: we use basic anonymous analytics to monitor website performance and traffic. This never includes access to your files or their contents.
  • Third-Party Cookies: Standard web analytics or advertising services may use cookies to serve better content. You can manage these in your browser settings.

By using PixelResize, you can be 100% confident that your sensitive documents, photos, and professional assets remain entirely under your control.
